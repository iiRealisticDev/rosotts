Rosott's Affiliate Applications

Requirements
  • Before affiliating with Rosott's one of the members of the Community Management Team must inspect such community to make sure they are abiding by our affiliation rules.
  • The community applying to ally with Rosott's must have an impeccable (clean) record and maintain professionalism at all times.
  • The community is to must have a minimum of 1,000 group members that are not bots.
  • The community must have sufficient/adequate amount of players during sessions.
  • The community must have an original game or assets under their corporation.
  • The community must have a discord server that displays professional announcements, development updates, sessions, and activity.
Perks
  • Access to our Monthly Partnership newsletter which keeps you up to date with our news and events!
  • A unique role in our Discord displaying your affiliation with us.
  • Get our affiliation announced in our Discord including the important links of your community.

To apply, fill out the form below. We will contact you via Discord if you are accepted.
The current estimated wait time is 1 week.
