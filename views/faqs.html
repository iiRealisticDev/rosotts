Frequently Asked Questions

Welcome to the Featured Frequently Asked Questions Section! The most relevant questions displayed here are professionally/accurately answered by the corporate staff.


Q: Does Rosott's provide positions?

A: Yes we do, and you may apply at the application center.

Q: When do updates come out?

A: Updates typically come out every now and then, sneak peaks are usually provided by the selected developers we have.

Q: How can I become a tester?

A: Our Bot & Web Dev Team is in charge of hiring testers. (The people in charge of this are Jack & Realistic)

Q: Does Rosott's sell purchasable ranks?

A: Yes we do sell ranks, but we only sell LR to preserve quality of our staff team.

Q: Can I help develop?

A: We will more than likely contact you if we ever need developers, other than that DM me if you're interested in developing.

Q: How do I become a Community Moderator?

A: Moderation positions will only be obtainable if the Chief Moderation Officer decides to open applications.

Q: How do I rank up with chat activity?

A: We currently do not offer any exclusive chat rewards for activity, we may in the future.

Q: How can I become/apply for the affiliate program?

A: You can always find our application here. We may also announce it in the Discord server

Q: How can I report a user breaking a rule?

A: You can open a ticket by going to commands and running the .ticket command. After you will be directed to a prompt. If it's urgent dm an online Community Moderator or an SHR.
